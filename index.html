    Gallery

    Give a fresh look to your new tab page. Be inspired with the beautiful backgrounds, inspirational quotes, subtle weather forecasts, set reminders, to-do lists and much more.

    Features

    • Background Photos

      Refreshing new background photos for your new tab page. Pick whether you want a new photo every day, hour or always.

    • Weather Updates

      Get updated with weather conditions and a quick look of the forcast. Pick temperature displays as Celsius or Fahrenheit.

    • Inspirational Quotes

      Get inspired with a motivational quote each time you open the tab.

    • Todo List

      Keep track of things with this movable widget. Choose how long to keep the completed items for your reference.

    • Clock

      So you like to see a big digital clock on your screen with current date details. We got you covered :)

    • Customizable Settings

      There are tons of customization options available for the extension. Configure a minimaliztic view or to your likings.
